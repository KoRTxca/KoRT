The Page

Free

The Page tier is designed for claimants in immediate crisis who need fundamental advocacy support without financial barriers. It provides access to the core KoRTX tools to ensure no one gets left behind.

Included Features

  • 1 Active Case: Manage one complete case file.
  • Template-fill Drafts: Access to basic statutory letter templates (e.g., initial claim filing, CL753 travel expense forms).
  • Basic Evidence Upload: Store up to 100MB of essential documents.
  • Merlin Local: Talk to the Merlin AI assistant for basic procedural guidance.
  • Community Support: Access the KoRTx community forums.
